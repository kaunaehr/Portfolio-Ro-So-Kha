Presenting a reimagined, redesigned album cover for the 1985 Tears for Fears album titled, 'Songs from the Big Chair'. The therapy chair, designed on Blender, is placed in the center, inspired by the television series Sybil, which the artists mentioned as the inspiration behind the album's title. The colors behind the chair are associated with each of the 8 songs, creating a visually captivating and meaningful design. The background contains a subtle protest image blended in to convey the overall mood of the album.
